About

In 2013, I committed Asian American treason and withdrew from the University of California, Irvine to instead attend California State University, Fullerton. Transferring universities this way had been considered a backwards step by many. This move meant I would be leaving behind a prestigious university, temporarily switching from devoted scholar to dropout, and wasting away years of my previous efforts. Fortunately, this move actually meant giving myself the opportunity to finally explore what I wanted for myself and how I wanted to get there.

In the coming years, I managed to work myself towards financial independence, independent living, and into a Computer Science program. I had the opportunities to work as a store manager, data processing operator, IT intern, and a "role model" (instructor) all while progressing through courses. These experiences gave me unique opportunities to grow my skill set as well as myself as a person. In Fall of 2019, I finished my final courses and am looking forward to continuing my trend of pursuing opportunity to have a life I can be excited for.

Regardless of what the future may bring, I plan on bringing myself up to the challenge as best I can. I will continue to learn and grow both as a working professional and as a person. Thank you for taking the time to read my About Me and I wish you all the best. Cheers!

This project is a Turing Machine version of the Game of Life. It is designed to take single step movements to create progression through the Game of Life. The code offers the ability to view the Turing Machine do every step-by-step calculation or to have each Game of Life turn to be displayed instead. This project heavily relied on translating basic computations into a workable game.

Creating this project was an interesting experience in terms of having to design game code to match the limitations of a Turing Machine. I will never forget my professor's adamant admissions of a Turing Machine being as powerful as any other computer possible.

The Turing Machine may be found here: Game-of-Life
